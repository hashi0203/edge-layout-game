Goal: Connect 4 corners by moving and rotating branches

How to use:

  • Select a branch by Click or Enter key
  • Move the branch by Drag or Up, Down, Left, Right keys
  • Rotate the branch by Drag or Left or Right keys with Control key

Colors:

  • Green dots: valid joints. Red dots: invalid joints
  • Invalid group: Red branches. Valid group: other colors.
